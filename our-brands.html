Viswanathan Pillai Kitchen Private Limited (VPK)

At the forefront of Bengaluru's FMCG and restaurant revolution, VPK is a leading accelerator, driving growth and innovation in India's food sector. They specialize in identifying key market opportunities and developing unique product lines that benefit manufacturers and farmers, thus boosting India's economy. Committed to excellence, VPK meticulously curates high-quality, industry-leading products to offer global consumers an experience of India’s rich culinary heritage and premium flavors.

Kalyani Oota

Kalyani Oota offers a unique dining experience inspired by the cultural heritage of Kalyani. This restaurant is uniquely designed as a step well, symbolizing the union of water and food. It's more than a dining place; it's a celebration of local traditions and cuisine. The menu features traditional, authentic dishes, with ingredients carefully sourced from local farmers, emphasizing sustainability and community support.
