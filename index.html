Welcome to Kalyani Oota

Welcome to Kalyani Oota

A unique dining experience nestled in the serene surroundings of Chikkaballapur, a 30-minute drive from Kempegowda International Airport (Bengaluru).

Our restaurant is more than just a vegan haven, it’s also a commitment to the joy of traditional food, the preservation of ancient temple tanks and step wells, and a celebration of centuries-old South Indian culinary traditions.

Introduction

Introduction

Discover Kalyani Oota, a unique dining experience nestled amidst the serene surroundings of Chikkaballapur, just a 30-minute drive from Kempegowda International Airport Bengaluru. Our restaurant is more than just a vegetarian haven; it's a commitment to the joy of traditional food, the preservation of ancient temple tanks and step wells, and the celebration of South Indian culinary traditions.

Our Mission: Rejuvenating Kalyani's

Kalyani Oota was born from a passionate endeavor to rejuvenate the ancient Kalyanis, sacred tanks which are central to local spirituality and community. Our dedicated team of volunteers works tirelessly with local communities and government agencies to restore these historic sites, preserving their physical and spiritual essence.

A Unique Dining Experience at the Entrance of Isha

A Unique Dining Experience at the Entrance of Isha

Inspired by the need to raise awareness of the Kalyanis' significance, we present Kalyani Oota, uniquely built in the concept of a step well. Located at the entrance of Isha and set against the backdrop of the "Kaurava Gunta" hillock, our restaurant provides a setting where water and food converge in harmony.
Our menu is a tribute to South Indian cuisine, featuring traditional and authentic dishes. We take pride in using locally harvested ingredients, supporting local farmers, and promoting sustainability. Each dish, from crispy masala dosas to aromatic filter coffee, promises an unforgettable journey through the region's gastronomic heritage.

Cultural and Community Integration

Kalyani Oota is a platform promoting the rich cultural heritage of Kalyani and Chikkaballapur. Choosing our restaurant means you are contributing to the revitalization of Kalyani. We dedicate a portion of our proceeds to restoring these sacred sites and regularly organize community events, workshops, and awareness campaigns.

A Special Experience

As you step into our rustic and inviting ambiance, perfect for a lazy Sunday brunch, a family gathering, or a weekend retreat, you become part of our mission. We invite you to experience the charm of ancient architecture, savor the flavors of authentic cuisine, and contribute to preserving our heritage.
